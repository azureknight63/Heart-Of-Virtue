# Heart of Virtue — Beta Tester Brief

Thanks for playing. Here's what you need to know before you start.

---

## What this is

Heart of Virtue is a text-based RPG set in a world called Aurelion. You play as
Jean Claire — a man in a coma, dreaming himself into the role of a crusader. The
game has a retro ASCII aesthetic and runs entirely in the browser.

This beta covers **Chapter 1 and the opening of Chapter 2**: from the Dark Grotto
through Verdette Caverns, ending when you defeat the Lurker. Plan for **45–60 minutes**
for a complete run.

---

## How to play

Use the **map panel** to move between tiles. Click NPCs to talk to them. In combat,
choose moves from the action panel — each move has a stamina cost and a cooldown.
The game will guide you through the first few moments.

If you get stuck, the game description text is your UI. Read it.

---

## The game ends intentionally

After you defeat the Lurker, a dialog will appear: **END OF BETA**. This is not a
bug. The road onward opens with the full release. You can keep exploring Verdette
Caverns after the dialog, but the main story stops there.

## Save warning — play in one sitting

Your progress is **not saved between sessions**. If you close the browser tab or
the server restarts, your run is lost. Finish in one sitting (~1 hour).

---

## How to give feedback

Use the **feedback button** near the account button, or
click **Send Feedback** in the end-of-beta dialog. Your report goes directly to
the development team.

The most useful things to tell us:

- **Bugs** — anything that blocked your progress or felt broken
- **Confusion** — moments where you didn't know what to do or why
- **Story reactions** — what landed, what didn't, what you felt
- **Combat impressions** — too hard, too easy, anything that felt off

You don't need to be thorough. One honest sentence beats a polished essay.
