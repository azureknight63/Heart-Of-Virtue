# Jambo
## Overview
- Name: Jambo
- Age: Middle-aged (appearance and bearing indicate many years of trading experience)
- Occupation: Apothecary-style merchant and shopkeeper (runs "Jambo Heals U")
- Appearance: Wiry, dark-skinned, usually wearing an oversized turban and a massive, easy grin. He moves with quick, economical gestures and often carries small cloth pouches and vials about his person.
- Personality: Warm, talkative, pragmatic, and market-savvy. Jambo is a born salesperson who pairs an infectious optimism with an honest, no-nonsense approach to commerce. He believes in treating customers with dignity and solving their problems rather than merely pushing wares.

## Important relationships
- Local adventurers: Regular customers who rely on Jambo for potions and quick cures.
- Suppliers and traveling merchants: Jambo's business depends on a rotating network of traders who bring him rare ingredients and curios.
- The town of Grondia: Jambo chose to set up his shop where travelers pass, and Grondia's bustle is integral to his livelihood.

## Role in the Story
Jambo functions as the friendly, dependable merchant archetype: a place where the player can resupply, gain small boons, and pick up gossip. His cheerful banter and business-savvy advice make him a memorable NPC who can also act as a minor quest-giver (finding lost merchandise, tracking a rare herb, or mediating a customer dispute). His shop can be a safe, low-stakes social hub between more dangerous scenes.

Mechanically, Jambo provides common consumables (Restoratives, Draughts, Antidotes) and maintains a small but reliable inventory. He offers helpful flavor dialogue and an exit message that underscores his theatrical persona: "Jambo wishes you well on your travels and don't forget: When you blue, Jambo Heals U!"

## Themes and Motifs
- Commerce and Community: Jambo represents how trade weaves into daily life and binds travelers, townsfolk, and information together.
- Practical Kindness: He balances profit with empathy — discounts for the needy, hard bargains for the pompous — and demonstrates that doing good can also be good business.
- Small Wisdom: His pocket-sized "Book of Business Wisdom" is a playful window into his ethos: honesty, presentation, and knowing your customer.

## Suggested In-Game Touches
- Items: A small pamphlet or readable book, "Jambo's Little Book of Big Deals," that grants minor charisma dialogue bonuses when quoted; a merchant's ledger which can start a fetch quest; a signature turban that slightly restores morale when worn.
- Encounters: Side-quests to help Jambo reclaim stolen stock, find a missing supplier, or test a batch of experimental restorative; optional persuasion checks to gain a discount.
- Dialogue Hooks: Short, flavorful lines where Jambo offers business advice, friendly jabs, or hints about local rumors and routes.

## Example Lyrical Snippet (for flavor)
> "Jambo set the bottles in a careful row like soldiers on parade, each one promising a small miracle. He winked and said, 'You don't buy things from me because you need them, friend — you buy them because you believe they'll make the next story worth telling.'"
