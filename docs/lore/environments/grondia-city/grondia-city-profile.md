# Grondia — City Profile

Overview
--------
Grondia is a vertical, stone-carved city built within and beneath a massive rocky bluff. It is a place of living chambers, market arcades, and ritual conclaves — a city shaped by generations of Golemite craft and careful excavation. Heat from ceiling crystals and mineral veins cut across the architecture, lending the city both a warm glow and a patient, ancient feel.

Key Districts
-------------
- Arcology: A vertical hive of carved living chambers, narrow walkways, and small bridges. The Arcology is dense with residences, stacked like terraces.
- Ecumerium: The market arcade and trade spine of Grondia. Stone counters and hanging banners advertise wares; this is where Jambo’s stall and other merchants ply their trade.
- Conclave: High vaulted ritual spaces and chambers of governance — pillars, mosaics, and ceremonial basins mark this area as the city’s spiritual and civic heart.
- Residences: Tight clusters of doorways and stacked thresholds create sheltered lanes where domestic life persists quietly behind carved lintels.
- Passages & Gateways: Grondia’s carved passages link districts and open onto deeper caverns (including routes to the Grondelith Mineral Pools).

Environment & Sensory Notes
---------------------------
- Stone and Mineral: The city is carved and polished stone; mineral veins and crystalline spires catch and refract light in shards of color.
- Sound: The city hums — distant trickles of water, the soft chime of crystalline echoes, and the low, continuous murmur of market voices.
- Smells: Resin, hearth-smoke, and the tang of wet mineral air in lower chambers.

Characters & Factions
---------------------
- Golemites: The city’s primary community — stoic stonefolk who maintain rituals around the mineral pools and shapes their civic life.
- Merchants: Jambo and others in the Ecumerium keep the trade flowing; itinerant traders and caravaners pass through frequently.
- Guardians & Ritualists: Conclave-attached figures and watchful guardians who care for ceremonial spaces and rare mineral sites.

Gameplay Hooks & Encounters
--------------------------
- Market Quests: Small fetch, appraisal, and negotiation tasks in the Ecumerium (supplies, rare herbs, vendor disputes).
- Ritual Duties: Missions tied to the Conclave’s upkeep or the protection of sacred sites (e.g., cleansing tasks, delivery of ritual items).
- Passage Hazards: Navigational puzzles and ambushes along narrow passages and bridges; environmental hazards from slick mineral runoff.
- Mineral Pool Access: Questlines and exploration routes that lead to the Grondelith Mineral Pools (see environment notes for the pools).

Map & Tile Notes
----------------

Design Guidance
---------------
- Preserve verticality: Grondia’s feel comes from stacked levels and layered sightlines — emphasize ledges, bridges, and terraces in any map art or level design.
- Light & Color: Play with warm, mineral-toned light sources (crystal glows) against cool stone shadows to create mood and contrast.
- Ritual vs Domestic: Mix sacred spaces (Conclave) with everyday life (Residences, Arcology) to ground high-concept moments in lived-in detail.
