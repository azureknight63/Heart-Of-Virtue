# Heart of Virtue — 2-Minute Leave-Behind

---

## A man is in a coma. He doesn't know it yet.

Jean Claire was an ordinary person — a tradesman, a husband, a father, a man of deep faith. Then his family was killed, and he survived, and the grief was too much to carry awake.

So his mind built him a world.

---

**Heart of Virtue** is a text-based adventure RPG set inside the coma-dream of a grieving man named Jean Claire. He wakes in a medieval world called Aurelion, believing himself a crusader. He doesn't remember his wife. He doesn't remember his daughter. His subconscious sealed the wound.

Your job is to help him face it.

---

### What Kind of Game Is This?

A **narrative RPG** in the tradition of Planescape: Torment — text-based, browser-playable, free to try. You'll explore a richly described world, form relationships with unusual companions, and fight your way through a tactical turn-based combat system with 70+ moves across a dozen weapon types.

But the story is what you're here for.

Jean's journey moves through the stages of grief without announcing them. His faith collapsed. His guilt is specific: *I chose Jerusalem. I planned the route. I put them there.* The question the game refuses to answer — "Why did God let this happen?" — is exactly the question real people carry. Heart of Virtue doesn't give you a resolution. It gives you Jean choosing to keep going anyway.
